Scoring

Critical Failures

  • Any image without alternative text that stops people from completing a task

Rating

Calculating the rating

  • The average of the adjectival rating of all the HTML Alternative Text Methods OR
  • The number of images in the document that pass divided by the total number of images in the document. Give the answer as a percentage. (This allows for automated testing of images.
Rating Scale
Rating 0 Less than 60% of all images have appropriate alternative text OR there is a critical failures on the path
Rating 1 60% - 69% of all images have appropriate alternative text and no critical failures on the path
Rating 2 70%-79% of all images have appropriate alternative text and no critical failures on the path
Rating 3 80%-94% of all images have appropriate alternative text and no critical failures on the path
Rating 4 95% to 100% of all images have appropriate alternative text and no critical failures on the path
