Scoring

Critical Failures

  • Any functional image without a text alternative is a critical failure.
  • Any informative image or image of text without a text alternative that stops people from completing a task on the path is a critical failure.
  • Decorative images do not have critical failures.

Rating

  • The average of the adjectival rating of all the HTML Alternative Text Methods OR
  • The number of images in the document that pass divided by the total number of images in the document. Give the answer as a percentage. (This allows for automated testing of images.)
Rating Scale for All Images
Rating 0 Less than 60% of all images have appropriate text alternatives OR there is one or more critical failures.
Rating 1 60% - 69% of all images have appropriate text alternatives and no critical failures.
Rating 2 70%-79% of all images have appropriate text alternatives and no critical failures.
Rating 3 80%-94% of all images have appropriate text alternatives and no critical failures.
Rating 4 95% to 100% of all images have appropriate text alternatives and no critical failures.
