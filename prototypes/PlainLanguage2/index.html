This new prototype for plain language is the result of comparing the examples from the original plain language experiment and devising a format that includes and expands on the WCAG Understanding document. It combines what will be normative and informative information. The decisions on what is normative and what is informative has not yet been made.

This set of tabs shows the generic outline for guidance. The Section Headings shows an example of what the outline looks like with guidance information. As of 12 October, not all of the information in Section Headings has been reviewed by a plain language specialist.
