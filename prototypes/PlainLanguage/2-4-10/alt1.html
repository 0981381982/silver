2.4.10 Section Headings: Section headings organize the words and images on a webpage. (Level AAA)

Note 1: "Headings" include titles and subtitles.

Note 2: This success criterion covers words and images, but not user interface components such as form fields, menus and checkboxes. Success Criterion 4.1.2 covers those.
